# Retail Sales Command Center

> **Interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to monitor retail sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product-level insights through KPI-driven reporting and interactive data visualization.**

---

# Project Overview

Retail organizations require continuous monitoring of sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product demand, and transaction trends to support informed business decisions.

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that transforms transactional sales data into actionable business insights using dynamic KPI reporting, trend analysis, geographic visualization, and interactive filtering.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to evaluate business performance across multiple dimensions including revenue, product models, brands, customer ratings, payment methods, and regional sales distribution.

---

# Business Objective

Develop an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard to:

- Monitor overall retail sales performance
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Evaluate product and brand performance
- Track monthly sales trends
- Compare payment method utilization
- Identify high-performing sales locations
- Support data-driven business decisions

# Dashboard Features

### Sales Performance Monitoring

Provides a consolidated view of retail sales performance through interactive KPI cards.

### Product Performance Analysis

Evaluates sales contribution across different mobile models and brands.

### Geographic Sales Analysis

Visualizes city-wise sales distribution using an interactive map.

### Monthly Sales Trend

Tracks monthly quantity sold to identify seasonal demand patterns.

### Customer Satisfaction Analysis

Analyzes customer rating distribution to understand product perception.

### Payment Method Analysis

Evaluates customer payment preferences across multiple transaction methods.

### Interactive Filtering

Supports dynamic filtering using:

- Brand
- Mobile Model
- Payment Method
- Day
- Month
